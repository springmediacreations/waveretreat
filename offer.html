What we offer

High-performance surf coaching, restorative yoga and unforgettable team adventures.

High Performance

Surf Coaching, Cornwall

From your very first wave to elite competition, we coach every level with patience, structure and stoke.

All surf coaches at WaveRetreat are qualified international beach lifeguards holding International Surf Association (ISA) Level 2 Surf Coach awards, combined with qualified yoga instruction to unlock maximum performance — and keep you safe in the water.

  • Private & one-to-one lessons
  • Group sessions and surf club
  • Beginner to elite performance coaching
  • Video analysis and technique feedback

Body & Mind

Yoga Practices

The ideal warm-up for a day of surfing — and a way to reduce the risk of injury.

Yoga teaches flexibility, balance, body control, breathing, focus and relaxation — everything that makes a better surfer. That’s why we offer two daily yoga sessions on every retreat. If you’ve never tried yoga before, your stay with WaveRetreat is exactly the right time to start.

Sessions are led by qualified teachers — founder Rich Lacey is a 200-hour yoga teacher, and our lead yoga teacher Alysia Ray brings 500 hours of experienced training to every practice.

Corporate Team Days

Get your team out of the office and into the ocean.

A WaveRetreat corporate day blends surf coaching, breathwork and yoga into an unforgettable experience that builds trust, resilience and focus. From ice-bath breathwork resilience sessions to silent-disco beach yoga, we tailor the day to your team and your goals — all delivered by qualified, safety-first coaches.

  • Surf sessions for all abilities
  • Breathwork & ice-bath resilience
  • Beach yoga and mindfulness
  • Fully equipped and insured
