Welcome to WaveRetreat

Surf more.
Stress less.

Founded by surf coach Rich Lacey, WaveRetreat helps people transform their lives through surfing and yoga — building confidence in the water and calm out of it.

From your first whitewater wave on the Cornish coast to a sun-soaked retreat overseas, our qualified coaches and yoga teachers meet you wherever you are and help you progress, with safety and stoke at the heart of everything we do.

What We Offer

Surf, yoga, adventure

Coaching that builds confidence, yoga that builds resilience, and retreats that bring it all together.

Surf Coaching

Performance coaching from first wave to elite. Every WaveRetreat coach is a qualified international beach lifeguard with ISA Level 2 awards and yoga instruction.

Yoga Practices

Flexibility, balance, breath and focus — the ideal warm-up for surfing and a way to reduce injury. Two daily sessions on every retreat, whatever your level.

Surf & Yoga Retreats
