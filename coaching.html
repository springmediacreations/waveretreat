Every Ability

Find your level

Not sure where you fit? Tell us your experience and we’ll place you in the right session.

1

Beginner

Never surfed or just a few goes. We cover safety, paddling, popping up and catching whitewater waves — you’ll be standing up on day one.

2

Improver

You can stand and ride straight. We move you onto catching unbroken (green) waves, angling your take-off and trimming along the face.

3

Intermediate

Confident on green waves. We refine bottom turns, generating speed and reading the line-up so you surf with flow and control.

4

Advanced & Elite

Sharpen manoeuvres, wave selection and competition strategy with detailed video analysis and high-performance coaching.
