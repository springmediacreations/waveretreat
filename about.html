Our story

Our Founder

Meet Rich Lacey

A passionate surf coach on a mission to change lives through surfing and yoga.

It was during his time skateboarding and training for competitions that Rich learned to quieten his mind, overcome fear and reach his goals. He founded WaveRetreat to help others do the same — pairing world-class surf coaching with the calm and focus that yoga brings.

From a first whitewater wave to elite performance, Rich coaches every level with patience and stoke — and as a qualified yoga teacher, he weaves breath and movement into everything WaveRetreat does, both in Cornwall and on retreat.

What We Stand For

Our values

Care

Safety first, always. We look after each other and every guest in the water, so you can push yourself and progress with total confidence.

Solo traveller, surfer, yogi or pro team — everyone is welcome. Our small, dedicated crew shares the stoke at every step.
